About This Template

This template provides two distinct styles for your presentations:

  • Presentation Style (Default): A modern, clean 16:9 Beamer theme for standard academic and professional presentations. It is production-ready and has been used for conferences and research talks.
  • Portal Style: A 9:16 vertical version, suitable for mobile-first viewing.

Both styles feature a progress bar, flexible citation management with BibLaTeX, and support for advanced content like multi-page PDF figures.

Quick Start

After cloning this repository, follow these simple steps:

1. Update Metadata

\title{Your Presentation Title}
\author{Your Name}
\institute{Your Institution}
\date{\today}

2. Add Your Content

% Edit slides.tex
\begin{frame}{Slide Title}
  Your content here
\end{frame}

3. Compile

cd presentation
pdflatex keynote.tex
biber keynote
pdflatex keynote.tex
pdflatex keynote.tex
